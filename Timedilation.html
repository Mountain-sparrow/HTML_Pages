The River of Time

Imagine time as a river. In a flat, open plain, the water flows smoothly and consistently. This is time as we experience it in our daily lives.

Now, imagine putting a massive boulder in that river. The water has to rush around it, changing its speed and path. In the universe, massive objects (like planets) and extreme speeds act like those boulders. They bend and warp the flow of time itself.
